Galgotias Editorial Club

Purpose

The Club aims at both unleashing creative as well as developing intellectual writing capabilities of members drawn from GU student’s community. The club organizes inter college debate, budget analysis and analysis on current economic issues which help to develop analytical skills among students. It involves students in editing the NDIM Journal “Review of Professional Management”. The Journal has ISSN number and listed in Ulrich Periodical Directory by Pro Quest, USA. Members of editorial club also publishes students weekly magazine Fiscal wholly on their own initiatives.

Mission

Mission of the club is to inculcate writing skills both on literary and business analysis, thinking and producing original works in original style. It has the mission of creating healthy competitive spirit among GU students and extended students community on Pan India basis. This helps developing networking facilities among students community.

Activities

  • Inter college debate and case study competition
  • Union Budget Competition
  • Editing College magazine
  • Preparing Booklets for seminars and conferences
